What is Block Cycle Labs?

Block Cycle Labs (BCL) is a DeFi protocol that lets holders of the $CYCLE ERC-20 token stake their tokens directly on-chain and earn fixed annual percentage yield (APY) paid from the protocol's reward pool.

The protocol runs on Ethereum Sepolia Testnet — a public test environment that mirrors mainnet behaviour. All transactions are gasless (using Sepolia ETH) and fully on-chain.

Quick Start

Get staking in under 3 minutes by following these steps.

  1. 1
    Install MetaMask — Download the MetaMask browser extension at metamask.io and create or import a wallet.
  2. 2
    Add Sepolia Testnet — In MetaMask, go to Settings → Networks → Add Network and add Sepolia, or switch via the network dropdown and select "Show Test Networks".
  3. 3
    Get Sepolia ETH — Visit sepoliafaucet.com and request free testnet ETH for gas fees.
  4. 4
    Get $CYCLE tokens — Open the DApp, click Buy $CYCLE in the nav to access the Swap interface and acquire CYCLE tokens.
  5. 5
    Stake — Enter the amount you wish to stake in the Stake panel, approve the transaction in MetaMask, and start earning rewards immediately.
💡 Rewards accrue in real-time and can be claimed at any time. There is no minimum staking period.

Staking $CYCLE

Staking locks your $CYCLE tokens in the BCL Staking contract and starts earning you a 12% fixed APY, credited continuously.

How to Stake

  1. 1
    Connect your wallet using the Connect button in the navbar.
  2. 2
    Navigate to the Stake tab in the action panel.
  3. 3
    Enter the amount of CYCLE you want to stake. You can use the MAX button to stake your entire balance.
  4. 4
    Click Approve to let the staking contract spend your tokens (one-time approval per session).
  5. 5
    Click Stake and confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your position is live immediately.

Claiming Rewards

Rewards accumulate every second based on your staked balance. You can claim at any time:

  • →
    Click the Claim Rewards button in the Stake tab or on your staked asset card.

Unstaking

You can unstake any amount at any time. Unstaking returns your principal plus any unclaimed rewards in a single transaction.

⚠️ Unstaking requires a Sepolia ETH gas fee. Always keep a small amount of Sepolia ETH in your wallet.

Reward Calculation

Rewards are calculated linearly over a 365-day year:

Daily Reward = (Staked Amount × 0.12) ÷ 365 Hourly Reward = Daily Reward ÷ 24 Per-Second = Hourly Reward ÷ 3600

For example, staking 1,000 CYCLE earns approximately 120 CYCLE/year, or ~0.329 CYCLE/day.

Swap

The Swap tab integrates with Uniswap V3 on Sepolia to let you exchange CYCLE ↔ WETH directly within the DApp.

How to Swap

  1. 1
    Click Buy $CYCLE in the navbar or navigate to the Swap tab in the action panel.
  2. 2
    Enter the amount of CYCLE you want to sell. The estimated WETH output updates in real-time.
  3. 3
    Review the rate and 0.5% slippage tolerance displayed below the inputs.
  4. 4
    Click Swap CYCLE → WETH. The DApp handles the approval automatically if needed.
ℹ️ If no CYCLE/WETH pool exists on Sepolia yet, the Swap tab will display a warning and the Pool tab will let you create one on Uniswap.

Slippage & Price Impact

The default slippage tolerance is 0.5%. For low-liquidity pools on testnet, price impact may be higher. The quoted output is an estimate from Uniswap's QuoterV2 contract.

Send

The Send tab lets you transfer $CYCLE tokens directly to any Ethereum address.

  1. 1
    Switch to the Send tab in the action panel.
  2. 2
    Enter the recipient's wallet address (must start with 0x).
  3. 3
    Enter the amount of CYCLE to send. Use MAX to send your full balance.
  4. 4
    Click Send CYCLE and confirm in your wallet.

Liquidity Pool

The Pool tab shows the status of the CYCLE/WETH Uniswap V3 pool on Sepolia and provides a direct link to manage liquidity.

CYCLE / WETH · 0.3% Fee Tier

Liquidity providers earn 0.3% of every swap through the pool. LP tokens remain in your wallet — BCL never custodies your funds.

To add liquidity, click Manage Liquidity on Uniswap in the Pool tab. You will be directed to the Uniswap V3 interface on Sepolia.

FAQ

Is the BCL DApp safe to use?

The DApp runs on Sepolia Testnet, which uses test tokens with no real monetary value. Smart contracts are verified on Etherscan and interactions are fully on-chain. Never send real ETH to Sepolia addresses.

Can I lose my staked tokens?

No. Your staked CYCLE tokens are held in the non-custodial staking contract. Only you can unstake them. BCL Labs has no admin key to move user funds.

Why do I need Sepolia ETH?

Every on-chain transaction (stake, unstake, claim, swap, send) requires a small gas fee paid in Sepolia ETH. Get free Sepolia ETH from sepoliafaucet.com.

When are rewards updated?

The DApp displays a real-time reward counter that increments every second based on your staked balance and the 12% APY. The on-chain claimable amount updates per block.

Is there a minimum stake amount?

No minimum. You can stake any amount of CYCLE, including fractional amounts.

The swap shows "No liquidity pool found" — what does that mean?

The CYCLE/WETH pool on Uniswap V3 Sepolia may not exist yet or may have insufficient liquidity. Use the Pool tab to create or add liquidity to the pool on Uniswap.

Will BCL launch on mainnet?

BCL is currently in testnet phase. Follow official announcements on the BCL website and social channels for mainnet launch details.
